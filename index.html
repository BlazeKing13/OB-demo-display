// Who Are We

BORN IN
THE STREETS.
BUILT FOR
ALL.

OpenBlock started in a Ljubljana garage. A few people who skated, rode, and snowboarded — and couldn't find clothes that matched what they actually did or how they actually lived.

So we made our own. No corporate input. No trend forecasting. Just gear designed from the pavement up, for the people who crack it before 8am and don't stop until they have to.

The brand is for everyone. If you ride, great. If you just love the culture and the aesthetic, that's equally fine. You belong here either way.
